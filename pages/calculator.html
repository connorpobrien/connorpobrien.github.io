Explanation:

These tools are designed to help powerlifters make informed decision during their training. The output values are intended for implementation with barbell squat, bench, or deadlift.

The first tool, the 1 Rep Max Calulator, is used to estimate your max strength for a lift based on a set performed with multiple reps. This is useful since frequent 1rm attempts cause unnessary fatigue and increase risk of injury.

The second tool, the Load Calculator, is used to guage what weight to use for an uncoming set. This is beneficial for lifters that are following an RPE based powerlifting program.
